# Sunad OTT — Fixed Programming Chart (FPC)

**Companion document to:** `02_PRD.md`, `07_Information_Architecture.md`, `08_Content_Strategy.md`
**Purpose:** Define the 24-hour linear scheduling strategy (Fixed Programming Chart) and weekly specials for the Sunad Broadcast TV/OTT Channel.

---

## 1. Programming Philosophy

The Sunad Broadcast linear TV and OTT channel offers a curated mix of **Knowledge + Entertainment + Mystery + Live Interaction + Folk Culture (ज्ञान + मनोरंजन + रहस्य + लाइव इंटरैक्शन + लोक संस्कृति)**. 

The Fixed Programming Chart (FPC) is designed based on contemporary Indian viewer preferences, digital trends, and a long-term retention strategy to maximize daily active engagement while preserving the premium civilizational positioning of the brand.

## 3. Weekly Specials (साप्ताहिक विशेष)

To provide variety and build anticipated weekly viewing routines, specific themes are assigned to each day of the week, culminating in a premium feature documentary on Sunday.

- **सोमवार (Monday):** शिव विशेष (Shiva Special)
- **मंगलवार (Tuesday):** हनुमान एवं भैरव (Hanuman & Bhairav Special)
- **बुधवार (Wednesday):** वेद विज्ञान (Vedic Science Special)
- **गुरुवार (Thursday):** गुरु परंपरा (Guru Parampara Special)
- **शुक्रवार (Friday):** शक्ति साधना (Shakti Sadhana Special)
- **शनिवार (Saturday):** तंत्र एवं नाथ परंपरा (Tantra & Nath Parampara Special)
- **रविवार (Sunday):** Mega Documentary — 2-Hour Premium Feature (Mega Documentary - 2 घंटे)

## 5. Strategic Content Pillars

To establish Sunad Broadcast as a nationally recognized, premium knowledge-based brand, content must be developed and delivered across three structural tiers:

- **Daily Shows:** Focus on building viewer habits and maintaining high daily active users (DAUs) using the standard FPC grid.
- **Weekly Flagship Programs:** Premium releases (like the Sunday Mega Documentary or prime-time exclusives) that build anticipation throughout the week.
- **Mega Event Programming:** High-impact broadcasts scheduled around major national/cultural milestones (e.g., Mahashivratri, Navratri, Kumbh Mela, Char Dham Yatra, Ram Navami, Guru Purnima) with 4–8 hour continuous live streams and special documentaries.
